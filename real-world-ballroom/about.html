Our Story

Get to know who we are, and why we do what we do.

Greetings! My name is Trey and I am a web developer. I am learning the skills to develop amazing web projects! I decided to startup my own web development firm while finishing my associates degree in web technologies at Forsyth Technical Community College. I am a native of Asheboro, NC and I am currently residing in Winston Salem, NC. I currently work in tech support role in the healthcare industry here in Winston. I live with my fiance, Courtney, and we've been together since 2019.

I enjoy a morning pot of coffee and time with my two dogs. I also enjoy games of all sorts. Party games, strategy games, card games, and the occasional video game. I find pleasure in many genres of music and I love going to live events. I listen to many sub-genres of rock, reggae-rock, and metal.

I found my love for web development all the way back in 1997 and even developed a few websites for companies in the time before Y2K. Today I am prepared to finish my associate degree in web technologies and I will graduate in May 2024.
